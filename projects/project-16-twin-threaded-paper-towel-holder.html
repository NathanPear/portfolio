PROJECT 16 / MECHANICAL

Twin-Threaded Paper Towel Holder

A SolidWorks table-mounted paper towel holder that uses twin threaded brackets and removable 3D-printed bolts to create a smooth towel dowel.

  • Role: CAD design and prototyping
  • Focus: threaded mounting, serviceability, and stiffness
  • Constraint: keep the towel bearing surface smooth

Project Details

Problem and Context

The design goal was to make a paper towel holder that could mount to a table securely, be easy to remove by hand, and present a smooth resting surface for the roll itself.

That meant the project had to solve both attachment and usability: strong enough to clamp in place, but simple enough to service without tools.

Scope and Constraints
  • Twin threaded brackets screwed into a table
  • Large 3D-printed bolt threaded into the bracket
  • Threads only near the bolt head so the towel rests on a smooth minor diameter
  • One bolt can be unthreaded by hand to remove the towel roll

Approach

What I Built
  • A pair of threaded brackets that fasten to the table edge
  • A large 3D-printed bolt that acts as the removable dowel support
  • A ribbed structural section instead of a solid mass to save material
  • Countersunk holes so the wood screws sit flush
Cross-Disciplinary Read

This is a good mechanical portfolio piece because it blends design intent, manufacturability, and user interaction. The thread geometry matters, but so does the service experience when someone removes the roll by hand.

The ribbed structure and countersunk mounting details also show that the design was made with material efficiency and assembly quality in mind, not just shape.

Outcome

Result

The finished design mounts to a table with twin threaded brackets, supports the paper towel roll on a smooth cylindrical surface, and can be serviced by unthreading one bolt by hand.

That gave the project a clean balance of rigidity, usability, and material efficiency.

Takeaways
  • Small details like thread placement can improve everyday usability
  • Ribs are often better than solid material when stiffness is the goal
  • Flush mounting hardware makes the final assembly feel more polished
